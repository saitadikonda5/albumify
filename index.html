Albumify

Top Track Generator

Wondering what your current favorite songs are? Spotify only shows you this information once every December. Use this website though to view your most streamed songs whenever you want! Albumify uses the Spotify API to retrieve listening data and then display your morst streamed songs as a personalized album tracklist.



How to use:
1. Log in using Spotify
2. Select the duration for your most streamed songs
3. View or download your results
